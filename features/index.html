Features

App + Hardware
We don't want to leave you hanging, our lock and keychain module comes with a custom app which will ensure that you recieve information from the lock when you need it.
Quick Installation
You love your door knob, and you value your security but you don't want to spend hours on it. We value you. Which is why our module can be installed with 10 minutes of your time and 5 quick steps.
WiFi Connected
To ensure that you recieve the information on time, with the help of Particle.io we have ensured that the device can be connected to your home WiFi network, thus securing the data and reducing the latency.
No Maintenance
Lastly, this module + keychain set comes with a gurantee of zero maintenance, once its installed, the only other time you would have to open the knob would be if you wanted to change locks. Oh! and don't worry, that new lock you install? We will be compatible with it too.
